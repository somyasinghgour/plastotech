Know Us

LAMINATED TUBE

Laminated tubes are widely used for packaging in personal care, food, pharma and industrial applications globally. Oral Care itself contributes to almost 70% of the total production of laminated tubes, even as the demand in cosmetics is also increasing fast.

Learn more

EXTRUDED TUBES

These seamless plastic tubes represent an exciting frontier in our industry, especially for the cosmetics industry, enabling superb aesthetics value and easy adaptability to several decorative forms. United Plastotech has been innovating furiously on extruded tubes to several sectors, bolstered by our acquisition of Arista Tubes, a leading manufacturer in Indore, INDIA.

Learn more

DISPENSING SYSTEM

United Plastotech offer a range of dispensing systems for all kinds of product applications. Apart from the ready options, we are happy to design new systems against specific client requests. We are keen to see dispensing systems making a big difference to client's products and will stay at the forefront of product development in this area.
