Who We Are

We United Plastotech & Engineering are the leading Exporte, Manufacturer and Supplier of Plastic Caps, Aluminium Collapsible Tubes, laminated and plastic tubes and plastic packaging material with a strong focus on quality, customer service and ethics.

This idea guides everything we do. It is about boldly challenging conventions and creating innovative solutions, with creativity, confidence, and co-creation. About helping our customers and their brands win in ever newer ways, that will set new benchmarks.

Tubes For Pharmaceutical Product Packaging : Cream, Ointment, Gel, etc For Cosmetics Product Packaging : Face Wash, Face Gel, Face Cream, Lip Balm, Face Scrub, Face Mask, Face pack, Day Cream, Night Cream, Moisturizer, Foundation, Concealer, make up remover, Nail paint etc For Personal & Oral care Product Packaging : Toothpaste, Shampoo, Shaving Cream, Shaving Gel, Serum, Body Lotion, Body Wash Gel etc For Painting Color Product Packaging : Acrylic Colors, Water Colors For Food Product Packaging : Jam, Mayonnaise, sauce, Pickle etc For Automobile, House Hold and Pest Control Product Packaging : Grease, Lubricants, Rat killer Paste, Cockroach Killer Paste, Adhesives etc.
